Three Engines

Power & Electrification

VST · CEG · NRG

The AI/data-center load growth is a multi-year demand shock against a supply-constrained grid. Independent power producers and nuclear operators with direct hyperscaler PPAs convert scarcity into durable cash flows. Highest structural conviction.

Profitable Platforms

UBER · AXON · TRMB · INTU · DASH

Software-and-services models with rising recurring revenue, real buybacks, and earnings compounding faster than sales. Most were de-rated in the growth sell-off while fundamentals stayed intact — the cleanest "quality on sale."

Medtech

PODD · BSX

Recurring, demographically-driven demand. PODD is high-growth execution (Type-2 TAM ahead); BSX is a contrarian repricing of a premium franchise after a guidance reset. Pair sizes the growth/value barbell.

Discipline: What We Cut

Highest upside ≠ highest conviction

Charter Communications (CHTR) screened with the largest implied upside in the S&P 500 (~80%) — yet carries only a Hold consensus (rating score 2.90). A wide target gap paired with weak analyst conviction usually signals a value trap or a contested turnaround, not a bargain. It is excluded by design.

Also de-prioritized: names where the upside came purely from a price collapse without an intact growth narrative. Big margins to target are necessary, not sufficient — conviction needs the fundamentals to corroborate the gap.
